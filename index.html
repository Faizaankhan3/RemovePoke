RemovePoke

Recently with launch of PokemonGo, our Facebook timeline got flooded with Pokemon related posts. But There are many people who is not interested in the Pokemon. And they hate their timeline being flooded with such post.

This Chrome extension will simply block all such posts. There is no configuration.

You can simply Download the extension from here and Add to your chrome browser (Instruction to add is given below), or you can clone the project and load into the chrome by turning developer option on.

To add the extension first download the crx file from the given link. Then open your download folder and the chrome extension page at chrome://extensions/ Drag and drop the file into the chrome extension page.

Then a popup will ask to add extension. Just click on "Add Extension" and you are ready to go.
